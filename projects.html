My projects

One of the projects I implemented in my studies in the Netherlands was a strategic communication project on fast fashion reduction. I spent 2 months analyzing world-known companies like Shein, Yves Saint Laurent, and Patagonia, focusing on their branding, production and target audience. Using all needed and provided material I came up with a 5-page strategically planned presentation on working with the reduction of Shein consumption and responsible consumption of Yves Saint Laurent and Patagonia.

One of the team communication projects I made was a “Dopper Wave”. This project is a collaboration with a sustainable Dutch company that produces multifunctional water bottles that you can order online. This production is really popular in the Netherlands, however, the company needed some help with a marketing plan with a set of events. We, as a team of 4 people, successfully came up with 5 promotional events for this company, and one of these ideas was a collaboration with Pantone for a new color palette.
